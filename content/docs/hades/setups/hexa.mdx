## Triggers 40% - 15%

### Iridis Liberatio - 40%

> 6 hit to all allies (1000% DMG total) + 3,000 plain DMG per stack  
> Gains a lot of buffs

Tank with 100% Cut or 1T Dodge or Rei Cold Stare.

### Sky Dragons' Trial - After Iridis Liberatio

> Similar to Bubz / SUBHL 50%, stays still for 6 turns.  
> Also deals 10,000 plain DMG per turn

Do 18 TA or 18 CA in 6 Turns. **MUST CLEAR**  
By clearing this omen, one of the buffs from the previous trigger will be removed.

<MDXCallout title="Omen Cancel" type="danger">
	Do not use Seasplitter / Siete S3 / Yuni S3 / 000 to clear this omen. Doing so
	will cause the buff to stay and this makes the raid deadly.
</MDXCallout>

### Lucentum Cladem - N

> 2 hit big DMG to random allies (9000% DMG total), dispel all buffs, inflict `Paralyzed`

40 hits to cancel.

### Atrophi Impetus - N

> 2 hit big Dmg to random allies (1000% DMG per hit), delay skill cd and reduce bar, reduce FC bar by 30%

25m DMG to cancel.

> 2 hit big DMG to random allies (6000% DMG total), inflict `Suffocate` and `Putrefied`, reduce FC bar by 30%

### Causticae Lacerationes - N

Dispel 3x to cancel.
