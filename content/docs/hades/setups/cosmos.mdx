### Sweeping Detonation - 75%

> Nullifies DMG taken while this omen is active.  
> 15-hit DMG to random allies (6000% DMG total / Can't be dodged / Ignores 'All-Elemental DMG Cut').  
> Inflicts `Shadowed` and `Supplemental DMG` to all allies.
> Gains `Uplifted`, `ATK Up` and `DEF Up`.
> Set bar to Red Lvl 8
