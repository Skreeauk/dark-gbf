## Triggers 100% - 51%

### Termination Flare - N (100% - 76%)

> 23 hits to random allies (3500% DMG total). Inflicts `Burned` and `Petrified`

15m DMG to cancel.

### Daedalus Drive - 75%

> 40,000 plain DMG to all allies, inflicts `Strong Armed`

Deal 10m DMG to cancel.  
Its really easy to deal 10m DMG.
Strong Armed can be annoying as it blocks healing but Death Grace DoT heal still goes through.

### Empyreus Verse - N (75% - 51%)

> 4000% DMG to the lowest HP ally, also switches allies with backline like UBHL

20m DMG to cancel.

### Arcadia Omen - Every 6T

#### Arcadia Foteinos

> 100% DMG, then gain buff that inflicts debuffs every turn

Land 10 debuffs to cancel. **Important to clear**.

#### Arcadia Skliros

> 100% DMG, then gain `DMG Cut Nullified`

Just ignore.

#### Arcadia Laimargos

> 100% DMG, then gain `Superior Elemental DMG`

Just ignore since usually theres Light Switch.

#### Arcadia Gnosis

> 100% DMG, then gain `Godsight`

Want to clear if your setup wants to dodge, but usually just ignore.

## Triggers 51% - 10%

### Omnipotent Cocoon - 51%

> Similar to Bubz 50%, Baha stays still for 5 turns. Baha gains a random stack each turn

Deal 50m DMG in 5 turns to cancel.

### Crisis Crunch - N

> 100% Max HP as plain DMG to highest current HP ally

20m DMG to cancel.

### Arcadia Omen - Every 6T

#### Arcadia Eclipse

> 100% DMG, caps your HP to 20,000

Deal 3,333,333 Plain DMG to cancel. E.g Bubz + Halmal.  
If Nighthound setup or any other setup without any Plain DMG, tank it and pray.

#### Arcadia Tromos

> 100% DMG, makes you unable to guard

6 Ougi to cancel.  
It only inflicts Defenseless (Cannot guard), so not important and just tank through it.

#### Arcadia Apocryphus

> 100% DMG, makes you unable to summon

12 Skills to cancel.  
Just press your skills and use Qilin.

## Triggers 10% - 0%

### Sirius Origin - 10%

> 99,999 plain DMG to 4 allies. DMG reduced with fewer targets

Can be subbed, when subbed it becomes 99,999 plain DMG to the sub character.

### Genesis Nova - N

> 999,999 plain DMG to all allies

Usually is the party wipe. Can tank with All-Sub.

### Crisis Crunch - N after Genesis Nova

> 100% Max HP as plain DMG to highest current HP ally

20m DMG to cancel.
