## Triggers 100% - 51%

### Termination Flare - N (100% - 76%)

15m DMG to cancel the omen

### Daedalus Drive - 75%

Deal 10m DMG to cancel, its really easy to deal 10m DMG

### Empyreus Verse - N (75% - 71%)

Doesn't hurt, applies Summonless. Just tank it

### Arcadia Omen - Every 6T

The only one that is important is:

- Arcadia Foteinos (Land 10 debuffs)

## Triggers 51% - 10%

### Omnipotent Cocoon - 51%

Deal 10m DMG to cancel, its really easy to deal 10m DMG

### Crisis Crunch - N

20m DMG to cancel the omen

### Arcadia Omen - Every 6T

#### Arcadia Eclipse

Deal 3,333,333 Plain DMG with Bubz + Halmal.
If Nighthound setup or any other setup without any Plain DMG, tank it and pray

#### Arcadia Tromos

6 Ougi to cancel.
It only inflicts Defenseless (Cannot guard), so not important and just tank through it

#### Arcadia Apocryphus

12 Skills to cancel. Just use qilin

## Triggers 10% - 0%

### Sirius Origin - 10%

Sacc a Sub / All-Sub char

### Genesis Nova - N

999,999 plain DMG to all allies. 99% is the wipe. Can tank with All-Sub

### Crisis Crunch - N after Genesis Nova

20m DMG to cancel
