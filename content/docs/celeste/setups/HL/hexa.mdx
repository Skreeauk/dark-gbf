## Triggers 40% - 15%

### Iridis Liberatio - 40%

> Big DMG to all allies + 3,000 plain DMG per stack

Tank with 100% Cut or 1T Dodge or Rei Cold Stare

### Sky Dragons' Trial - After Iridis Liberatio

> Similar to Bubz / SUBHL 50%, stays still for 6 turns.  
> Also deals 10,000 plain DMG per turn

Do 18 TA or 18 CA in 6 Turns. **MUST CLEAR**

### Lucentum Cladem - N

> 2 hit big DMG to random allies, dispel all buffs, inflict `Paralyzed`

40 hits to cancel

### Atrophi Impetus - N

> 2 hit big Dmg to random allies, delay skill cd and reduce bar, reduce FC bar by 30%

25m DMG to cancel

> 2 hit big DMG to random allies, inflict `Suffocate` and `Putrefied`, reduce FC bar by 30%

### Causticae Lacerationes - N

Dispel 3x.
