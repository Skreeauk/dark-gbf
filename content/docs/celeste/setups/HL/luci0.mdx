## Triggers 100% - 80%

### Paradise Lost - Join

> 50,000 plain DMG to all allies

Make sure you have 50,000+ HP

### The End - 100%

> Party Wipe

Deal 30m DMG to cancel.  
Clear all apples if possible as well

### Iblis - N

> 5 hit to random allies. 6,000 plain DMG to all allies. Gain `DMG Cut` and `Echo`

Dispel 2x to cancel.  
If cannot cancel, then guard

### Phosphosrus - N

> 5 hit to random allies. 6,000 plain DMG to all allies. Inflict `Strong Armed` and `Blind`

Land 7 debuffs to cancel.  
Dark has easy access to 7 debuffs

### 95%

<Steps>
<Step>

#### Axion

> 3 hit to all allies. Inflicts `DATA Down` and lower bar

4 TA to cancel.

</Step>

<Step>

#### Ficus

> Big DMG to all allies. Inflicts `Slashed` and `Weakened`

20m CA DMG to cancel.

</Step>

<Step>

#### Sephiroth

> 12 hit to all allies. Inflicts `Skill Delay`

15m Skill DMG to cancel.

</Step>

</Steps>
